## General

### What is DarkMatterBank?
DarkMatterBank (DMB) is the next-generation AI payment infrastructure designed for autonomous AI agents. We provide programmable payment channels, multi-chain settlement, and enterprise treasury management that enables machine-to-machine transactions without human intervention.

### Why is DarkMatterBank needed?
AI agents are becoming autonomous, but they cannot pay autonomously with current DeFi infrastructure. Every transaction requires human signatures and manual approval. DarkMatterBank builds the financial rails that AI agents need to function independently.

### Who is DarkMatterBank for?
- AI companies building autonomous agents
- DeFi protocols needing cross-chain settlement
- Enterprises managing multi-chain treasuries
- DAOs requiring programmable governance funds

### What makes DarkMatterBank different from traditional payment rails?
Unlike traditional payment systems designed for humans, DarkMatterBank is built natively for AI agents. We support 15+ blockchain networks, sub-3-second settlement, and less than $0.001 transaction costs.
