## 📋 Brand Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- Always maintain clear space around the logo (minimum 1x logo height)
- Do not distort, rotate, or modify the logo
- Use on dark backgrounds for optimal visibility

### Color Palette
- Primary: #1A1A2E (Deep Space)
- Secondary: #16213E (Dark Matter)
- Accent: #7B2CBF (Purple)
- Highlight: #E94560 (Energy Red)
- Text: #FFFFFF / #E8E8E8

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, modern sans-serif
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Monospace for code/technical content
