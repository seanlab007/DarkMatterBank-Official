## AI Native Payment Infrastructure

---

## The Problem

**AI agents are proliferating, but they can't pay.**

Autonomous AI systems need to:
- Pay for compute resources
- Purchase data and APIs
- Settle between each other
- Manage enterprise treasuries

**Current DeFi infrastructure requires human signatures.**

Every transaction needs manual approval. Every wallet needs human custody. This is incompatible with autonomous AI workflows.

---

## Our Solution

**DarkMatterBank is the financial infrastructure for AI agents.**

We provide:
- **Agent Pay Protocol**: Programmable payment channels for autonomous execution
- **Enterprise Vault**: Multi-signature treasury management with KYÄ identity
- **Settlement Engine**: Sub-3-second cross-chain settlement across 15+ networks
- **AI Adapters**: Native support for OpenAI, Anthropic, and DeepSeek function calling
